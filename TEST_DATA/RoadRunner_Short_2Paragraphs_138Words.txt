By the end of the session, the group began referring to the initiative as RoadRunner, and the name stuck. Priya Singh will lead the program end to end, including coordination, steering checkpoints, and overall delivery. The kickoff for version 1.0 is set for October 29, 2025, with a pilot planned for mid-February 2026. The focus of this phase is to get the core platform working correctly before adding advanced features later.

The main goal is to build a unified customer service workspace for a national car-service brand. The current issue is that customer conversations move across chat, SMS, and voice in a fragmented way, which forces agents to adjust constantly and repeat questions. RoadRunner is meant to provide a single continuous interaction space. Priya described the intended outcome as calmer agents, faster customer help, and fewer repeated conversations.
